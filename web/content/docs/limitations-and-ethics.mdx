Selection Room is a transparent decision-support tool for exploring CFP selection under published rules.

## What this page covers

- What the simulator is and is not
- Known limitations
- Responsible use guidelines

## What Selection Room is

A tool to audit, explain, and stress-test ranking assumptions with reproducible inputs.

## What Selection Room is not

- Not an official CFP product
- Not a committee-vote replica
- Not a claim that the committee is wrong
- Not a substitute for human judgment

## Known limitations

1. Committee subjectivity is not fully captured in data
2. Injuries and availability are not modeled
3. Weights are configurable defaults, not secret committee weights
4. Min-max normalization makes scores relative to each run universe
5. Selection Stability varies weights only; it does not simulate future games
6. Historical validation is informative, not proof of replication

<Callout type="error" title="Warning">
Do not present model output as an official projection or claim unsupported bias without cited evidence.
</Callout>

## Non-affiliation

Selection Room is an independent analytics project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the College Football Playoff.
