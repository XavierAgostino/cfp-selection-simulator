# Case Study: 2023 Florida State

## Context

Florida State finished 13–0 and won the ACC but ranked lower in some metrics due to narrow wins and injury context (starting QB out late season). The committee still selected FSU; debate focused on whether an undefeated champion could be excluded under a purely metrics-driven system.

## Simulator relevance

- Tests how **automatic bid** logic interacts with low composite rank.
- In 12-team rules, a conference champion receiving an auto bid can **displace** an at-large team ranked higher.
- Injury data is **not** in the model; see [Limitations & Ethics](../limitations-and-ethics.md).

## Questions to explore

- Run validation for 2023 and inspect rank correlation.
- Under 2024 12-team rules, would FSU's auto bid trigger displacement?
